Thathva's Art Gallery

Welcome to our Thathva's Art Gallery gallery where we Transform ordinary Frames into extraordinary art pieces meticulously adorned with the natural beauty of stones and The captivating sparkle of crystals.

Our journey begins with fascination for the raw beauty of Gemstones and Hand Embroidery which brings natural beauty and magic into everyday living spaces transforming simple frames into statement pieces that tells a story.

Call to action or connect with us.

Explore our gallery of unique art and frames and find the perfect pieces to illuminate your space. follow us on social media links to see our latest creations and behind the scenes and glimpses.

The founder of this venture had a brilliant idea—to create unique photo frames featuring gemstones and intricate embroidery. These beautifully crafted frames are designed to enhance the aesthetic of your walls while remaining affordable. Whether it’s a portrait of a loved one, a divine image, nature scenes, or a custom design of your choice, we bring your ideas to life in stunning frames that truly shine. These frames also make thoughtful and memorable gifts for any occasion.
